About Our Project

The Illeret Natural Habitat Environmental and Restoration Transformation (IN-HEART) initiative rehabilitates degraded grasslands in Illeret Ward, Marsabit County.

We address environmental degradation through:

  • Tree planting and seed dispersal
  • Invasive species control
  • Community education programs
  • Ecosystem restoration

Our Programs

Seedball Dispersal Initiative

We distribute 24,000 seed balls annually containing native tree species and grasses to restore degraded areas. Each seed ball is protected by a bio-char coating that shields it from predators and extreme temperatures until the rains come.

Implementation:

  • Procure two 100kg bags of seed balls yearly
  • Partner with Seedballs Kenya for quality assurance
  • Disperse during both rainy seasons
  • Engage schools and community groups in dispersal

One Tree One Student (OTOS)

This initiative partners with Illeret High School and Elbokoch Primary School to donate tree seedlings to students. Each student owns and cares for their tree, fostering environmental stewardship.

Tree Species Distributed:

Neem Tree

(Azadirachta indica)

Medicinal properties, shade provider

Toothbrush Tree

(Salvadora persica)

Local name: Mti mswaki
